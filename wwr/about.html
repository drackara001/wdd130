Thunder Wave Expedition Company

Our company exists to bring adventure, joy, and connection to all who seek the thrill of the river. Our mission is to paddle boldly into new horizons, guided by courage and camaraderie. Our creed is to honor the waters, respect the wild, and cherish every splash of laughter. Our motto: "Row strong, live free."

History

Thunder Wave Expedition Company was founded in the early 1990s by a small group of outdoor enthusiasts who believed that the river could teach courage, teamwork, and joy. What began as weekend trips with borrowed rafts soon grew into a dedicated adventure company. Over the years, we expanded from a single raft on a local river to guided expeditions across some of the most thrilling white water routes in the region. Our legacy is built on safety, respect for nature, and a passion for sharing the rush of the rapids with adventurers of all ages. Today, White Wave Rafting continues to honor its roots—combining tradition, expertise, and a spirit of discovery in every journey.
